What IS 4 meals in 4 minutes™?

4 meals in 4 minutes™ is an easy, step-by-step preparation technique designed to help you have meals pre-prepared and ready to cook when you need them. It’s having meal CHOICES on hand instead of having to make meal decisions at the last minute!

How do I know what to put together?
Homemade Gourmet has created a handy meal planner, complete with grocery list, to take the “guess work” out of preparation! It includes simple recipes to create 4 delicious meals such as Garlic Lemon Chicken, Italian Chicken, Grandmother’s Sunday Roast and Garlic Basil Chicken.
