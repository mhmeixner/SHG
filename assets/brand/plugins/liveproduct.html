Get your products out there
Live Product Upload Beta is a free service that lets you, the online retailer, make your product catalog available via Live Product Search.

Easy, fast, effective (and free!)
This easy-to-use gateway helps you upload accurate and up-to-date product data directly into the Live Product Search index, in any supported format, including text or XML, through either HTTP upload or FTP.

Give customers compelling and complete info
Product information can include images, descriptions, prices, and links to your Web site. You can upload any number of catalogs. Once a catalog has passed the validation process, it will be searchable through Live Product Search.
