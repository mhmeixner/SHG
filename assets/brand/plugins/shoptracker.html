Shopping Feeds and Comparison Shopping Engine Product Feed Management Software!

ShopTracker is a central management point for all your products and shopping feeds. Through ShopTracker's interface, a client can update prices; add or remove products; change product categories; and create product feeds for comparison shopping engines (and automatically have the products mapped to the correct category at that CSE). ShopTracker then creates a new xml feed (daily or 2x per day, depending on the CSE's access terms), and sends it to the CSE. ShopTracker can also be configured to update product prices in your website DB, and/or fulfillment system!
