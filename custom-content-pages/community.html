Homemade Gourmet® is proud to help impact lives in a positive way by supporting the efforts of World Vision International through sales of Basic Bread Mix and by donating meals to the families featured on Extreme Makeover Home Edition. We feel it is important to give back to those in need and it is our honor to do so.

Millions of children across the world are forced to deal with the daily realities of hunger and hopelessness. Without access to healthy food, clean water, healthcare, education, and economic opportunities, these children and their families become trapped in an endless cycle of poverty and despair. Homemade Gourmet® is determined to break this cycle and bring hope back to these precious children through a partnership with World Vision. World Vision is an International Christian humanitarian organization who provides assistance for the world’s poorest children and families in nearly 100 countries around the world. Homemade Gourmet® donates a portion of the proceeds from the sale of each World Vision Basic Bread for Life Mix and joins with hundreds of Homemade Gourmet® Consultants, employees, and customers in sponsoring children in communities in India, Guatemala, Honduras and other countries across the world.



Homemade Gourmet® has also made a commitment to children in Malawi, Africa, through World Vision’s Hope Child Sponsorship. Homemade Gourmet® is striving to help meet the needs of these especially vulnerable children who have lost one or both parents to HIV/AIDS. In addition to sponsorships, in 2003, Homemade Homemade Gourmet® donated the funds to build a two-room schoolhouse in Asodeso, Guatemala. And in 2004, Homemade Gourmet®, World Vision and Helps International teamed up on another special project; a stove campaign. Hoping to prevent the thousands of accidents and deaths that occur every year due to unsafe open fire cooking pits, Homemade Gourmet® donated one clean venting stove for every three purchased through World Vision. The funding of special projects continues to be an ongoing aspect of our involvement with World Vision.

Currently Homemade Gourmet is campaigning to raise the funds to provide backpacks filled with school supplies for the children in our nation’s most distressed communities. With your help we can reach across the globe and touch those who are in need.

When requested, Homemade Gourmet® provides the families featured on Extreme Makeover Home Edition with a pantry full of products to help them create some sweet mealtime memories in their new home!

It is exciting to be a part of helping these deserving families as they receive a fresh start. Knowing that they can also enjoy an easy-to-prepare, great-tasting meal to go along with their new kitchen is our reward.

Hats off to the many volunteers and sponsors that help make these dreams come true!
