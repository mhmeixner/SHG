Sweet and Sour Chicken

Ingredients: 1  Homemade Gourmet® Italian Red Pepper Bread Dippin’ Mix

3  pounds boneless,  skinless chicken breasts
1  14-ounce can chicken broth

  1. cup brown sugar
gallon size freezer bag, if preparing ahead

Directions:
Make It Now- Place all ingredients into slow cooker.
Prepare For Later- Place all ingredients into a gallon size freezer bag. Seal and SMOOSH to blend ingredients. Label bag as Sweet and Sour Chicken along with slow cooking instructions and place in freezer until needed.
Slow Cook: Defrost enough to empty contents into slow cooker. Cook on low 6 hours or high 4 hours.
Serving Suggestion: Serve with steamed rice and steamed broccoli.
Note: Reserve 2 chicken breasts and 1 cup sauce if you wish to prepare the Bonus Meal.

Serves 6

Italian Round Steak

Ingredients:
1   Homemade Gourmet® Gusto Garlic Pesto Cheeseball Mix
4   pounds round steak, cut into strips
2   15-ounce cans diced tomatoes
1   cup instant rice
gallon size freezer bag, if preparing ahead

Directions:
Make It Now- Place all ingredients, except rice, into slow cooker.
Prepare For Later- Place all ingredients, except rice, into a gallon size freezer bag. Seal and SMOOSH to blend ingredients. Label bag as Italian Round Steak along with slow cooking instructions and place in freezer until needed.
Slow Cook: Defrost enough to empty contents into slow cooker. Cook on low 6 hours or high 4 hours. When round steak is done, remove ingredients for bonus meal if desired. Add rice, replace lid and cook 30 more minutes or until rice is tender.  
Serving Suggestion: Serve with green beans.  
Note: Reserve ½ of the cooked round steak and 1 cup sauce if you wish to prepare the Bonus Meal.

Serves 6

Pepper Steak Sandwiches

Ingredients:
1-2   pounds reserved, precooked round steak and 1 cup liquid from Italian Round Steak
6   Hoagie buns
1  green bell pepper, seeded and sliced into thin strips
1  medium onion, sliced into thin strips (optional)
shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions:

  1. Place cooked round steak, juice and bell pepper strips into a skillet. Heat all over medium heat until peppers are tender and meat is warmed through.
  2. Spoon equal portions of heated mixture into hoagie buns and top with mozzarella cheese.

Serving Suggestion: Serve warm with chips or fresh veggie sticks

Serves 6

Garlic Parmesan Chicken

Ingredients:
1   Homemade Gourmet® Garlic and Parmesan Bread Dippin’ Mix
3   pounds boneless, skinless chicken breasts
1  cup low sodium chicken broth
¼   cup heavy cream
gallon size freezer bag, if preparing ahead

Directions:
Make It Now- Place all ingredients, except cream, into slow cooker.
Prepare For Later- Place all ingredients, except rice, into a gallon size freezer bag. Seal and SMOOSH to blend ingredients. Label bag as Italian Round Steak along with slow cooking instructions and place in freezer until needed.
Slow Cook: Defrost enough to empty contents into slow cooker. Cook on low 6 hours or high 4 hours. When chicken is done, remove 2 chicken breasts for bonus meal if desired. Set aside remaining chicken breasts on a serving plate. Pour juice from slow cooker into a skillet over a medium-high heat. Add cream and stir until heated through. Reserve 1 cup cream sauce for bonus meal if desired. Pour this cream sauce over the chicken.
Serving Suggestion: Serve with rice and tossed salad.   
Note: Reserve 2 precooked chicken breasts and 1 cup cream sauce if you wish to prepare the Bonus Meal.

Serves 6

Quick and Easy Fettuccini

Ingredients:
2   precooked chicken breasts and 1 cup cream sauce from Garlic Parmesan Chicken
16  ounces fettuccini pasta (prepared according to package instructions)

Directions:
Dice the cooked chicken into small portions and heat with cream sauce over medium heat until warmed through. (add additional cream if sauce is too thick)
Toss with cooked pasta and serve warm.
Serving Suggestion: Serve warm with tossed salad.

Serves 6
