Cookies
Cookies are essential "invisible" data files that act as an electronic messenger between your computer and our website. Cookies enable us to save personal information about you on your computer. The most basic cookie tracks what is in your shopping cart. This saves you time, as you will not have to re-input your Personal Information each time you visit or shop with us. Cookies store your Personal Information for you. All Internet browsers have a notification option to let you know when an "invisible" cookie is being sent to you. Through your browser options you can choose not to accept the cookie. However, in not accepting the cookie, you may not be able to experience all functions of shop.homemadegourmet.com.

We may use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our servers and to administer our websites. Your IP address also may be used to gather broad demographic information and to recognize customer traffic patterns and site usage. This information aids us in merchandising and in developing the design and layout of the site. IP addresses are not linked to personally identifiable information.

Security
Whether you place an order or create a profile, we offer the use of a secure server. The industry-standard secure server software (SSL) that we use encrypts all information you input before it is sent to us. Our site is verified secured daily by GeoTrust QuickSSL. Sites secured by GeoTrust SSL certificates are providing up to 256 bit encryption thereby offering the highest level of encryption and security possible. This means you can rest assured that communications between your browser and this site's web servers are private and secure. Look for the GeoTrust banner toward the bottom left hand corner of this website to learn more.

To check the security of your connection, once you have reached checkout, look at your address ba, the first characters of the site address will change from "http" to "https."
